The supplied documents have been organized as a study route. They are not reproduced verbatim: their contents support the theory, practice, and exercises in this book.

Table 1 is a map: start with the mathematical tools, continue with PCA theory, and finish with practice and exercises.

## Key ideas included in the book

1. Centering moves the point cloud to its center of gravity.
2. Spectral decomposition describes the principal axes of symmetric matrices.
3. SVD connects the data matrix with the eigenvalues of covariance or correlation matrices.
4. PCA finds maximum-variance projections and supports dimensionality reduction.
5. Interpretation requires distinguishing weights, scores, loadings, explained variance, and quality of representation.
